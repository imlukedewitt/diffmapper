Summary Add Remove Option for Unused Meters

Adds ability to delete meters that aren't attached to any component, meter field, or formula meter. Gated by a can_destroy? check on the model, exposed through the serializer so the frontend can conditionally show the remove option.

Branch: codex/add-remove-option-for-unused-meters

Flow Overview
  • Backend: routes → controller#destroy → model#can_destroy? / check_destroyable
  • API: serializer exposes can_destroy boolean
  • Frontend: MetersTab → PinotDashboard → MeterChart (prop drilling onMeterRemoved)
  • MeterChart calls DELETE /metering/streams/:uid/meters/:id
